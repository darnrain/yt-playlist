Binding keyboard media keys for mpv.
These commands are for Linux.
Only works when yt-playlist is playing audio/video, unless you have mpvsocket enabled on your computer. 

Play/Pause command.
echo cycle pause | socat - "/tmp/mpvsocket"

Next track command.
echo playlist-next | socat - "/tmp/mpvsocket"

Prev track command.
echo playlist-prev | socat - "/tmp/mpvsocket"
